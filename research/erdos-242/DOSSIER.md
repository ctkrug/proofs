# Erdős #242 — hard lane dossier

Official source: <https://www.erdosproblems.com/242>

For every integer `n > 2`, seek distinct `1 ≤ x < y < z` with
`4/n = 1/x + 1/y + 1/z`.

Known starting constraints from the current source page:

- It suffices to consider prime `n`.
- Verified computationally through `10^18`; extending a blind bound alone is low-value.
- Mordell leaves six residue classes modulo 840; later work refines the exceptional classes.
- Productive runs should target a new parametric family, a rigorous congruence-cover improvement,
  a verified equivalence/reduction, or a reusable exact search harness.

Do not describe numerical verification as proof of the universal statement.
